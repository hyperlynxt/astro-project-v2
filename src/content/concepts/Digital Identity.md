Digital identity is the persona we construct through data points, social media posts, and online interactions. Unlike our physical presence, a digital identity is persistent, searchable, and often highly curated.

The tension between the messy reality of the physical self and the polished, data-driven avatar of the digital self is a central conflict in modern existence.
