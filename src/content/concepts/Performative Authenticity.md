Performative authenticity is the conscious effort to appear authentic, vulnerable, or 'real' in digital spaces. This performance is often driven by the 'feedback loop' of social media engagement.

In the digital world, even our most private moments become a stage. The irony is that the more we perform 'authenticity,' the further we may find ourselves from the messy, uncertain reality that characterizes true human experience.
