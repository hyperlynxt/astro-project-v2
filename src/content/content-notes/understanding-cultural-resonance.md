# Understanding Cultural Resonance

Cultural resonance is not just about popularity; it's about the **alignment** between a narrative and the pre-existing emotional landscape of a community.

## Key Observations
- Symbols are most resonant when they bridge the gap between **Tradition** and **Novelty**.
- Resonant narratives often act as "social glue" during periods of transition.

This concept maps directly to the **Interlinked & Intertwined** framework for social cohesion.
