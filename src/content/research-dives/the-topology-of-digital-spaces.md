# The Topology of Digital Spaces

Digital space is not "empty" space. It is a structured field of relations.

## Beyond the Desktop Metaphor
The transition from the **desktop metaphor** to the **cloud** has fundamentally altered our spatial navigation. We no longer "go to" a file; we "summon" it.

## Key Findings
- **Spatial Memory**: Persistent layout anchors improve retrieval times even in non-physical spaces.
- **Nodal Interfaces**: Graph-based navigation reduces cognitive load for complex datasets.
- **The Intangible Core**: Architecture without gravity requires a new set of **physicality heuristics**.
