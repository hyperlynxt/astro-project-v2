---
word: Run of the Mill
definition: "Ordinary, unremarkable — describing something that meets the minimum standard but offers nothing exceptional. From textile mills, referring to ungraded cloth fresh off the production run."
note: "The industrial origin is worth holding. The mill produced without judgment; quality was assessed after. 'Run of the mill' is things before evaluation — the neutral, the unexamined, the default."
tags: [Idiom, Register, Etymology]
stars: 2
date_added: 2024-10-12
