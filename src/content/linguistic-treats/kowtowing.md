---
word: Kowtowing
definition: "To act in an excessively subservient manner; to show servile deference. Originally from the Chinese custom of touching the ground with one's forehead as a sign of respect."
note: "What makes kowtowing such a sharp word is the physical memory inside it: the act of lowering oneself to the floor. Even used figuratively, it implies a soul that has voluntarily surrendered its height."
