---
word: Forgone
definition: "Past participle of forgo — to willingly abstain from or surrender something. Also appears in 'foregone conclusion': an outcome treated as already decided before examination."
note: "The 'foregone conclusion' usage is the more interesting case. It names a cognitive move: treating the future as already settled. Once something is foregone, scrutiny feels superfluous. The word is a trap."
