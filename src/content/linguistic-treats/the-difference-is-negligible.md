---
word: The difference is negligible
definition: "A phrase used to indicate that the distinction between two things is so small or unimportant that it doesn't warrant further consideration or concern."
note: "The word 'negligible' is the heart of this phrase. It doesn't mean there is *no* difference—it means there is a difference that we have decided to ignore. It is an act of forced indifference."
