---
word: Whataboutism
definition: "A rhetorical tactic that responds to an accusation or difficult question by raising a different issue, typically intending to deflect criticism by pointing to hypocrisy."
note: "A classic defensive maneuver. It doesn’t disprove the point; it just tries to redistribute the guilt. It’s the linguistic equivalent of pointing at a fire in the distance to ignore the one at your feet."
