---
word: That's Very Naff
definition: "British slang for something that is tacky, unstylish, or lacking in social grace. It describes a failure of taste that is both obvious and slightly embarrassing."
note: "To be 'naff' is to try too hard and get it wrong. It’s the aesthetic version of a joke that no one laughs at. It implies a lack of ease—a performance of style that has lost its way."
