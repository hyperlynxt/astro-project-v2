---
word: I am the Truth
definition: "A phrase asserting identity with truth itself — drawn from theological discourse but now used culturally to claim absolute authority or unquestionable authenticity."
note: "What's fascinating is the shift from 'I tell the truth' to 'I am the truth.' The first is epistemological. The second is ontological — it doesn't claim accuracy, it claims essence. A very different kind of assertion."
