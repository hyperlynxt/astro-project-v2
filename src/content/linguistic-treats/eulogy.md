word: Eulogy
definition: "A speech or piece of writing that praises someone highly, typically delivered at a funeral. From Greek eu (well) + logos (word) — literally: a good word."
note: "What moves me is the etymology: a eulogy is eu-logos, a well-word. It is language put in service of goodness, of making the invisible visible one last time. The word treats language itself as a final gift."
