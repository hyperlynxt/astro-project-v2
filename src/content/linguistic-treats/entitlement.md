word: Entitlement
definition: "The belief that one is inherently deserving of privileges or special treatment — but also, formally, a legal right to something. The word has shifted from neutral legal usage to a charged moral accusation."
note: "The semantic drift here is worth studying. 'Entitlement' once described a legitimate claim. Now it almost always implies an illegitimate one. The word has become a verdict. Whoever deploys it has already won the argument."
